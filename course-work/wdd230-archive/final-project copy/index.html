Who are we?

With roots going as far back as 1887, the Salt Lake City Chamber of Commerce knows the area, the businesses, and, most importantly, the people who make up our city. We believe in protecting and fostering business relationships, as well as our shared interests. We have a constant goal of increasing prosperity, employment, and community support for the jobs in this city. By joining us, you are serving your city, your employees, and your customers. The chamber consists of businesses of all sizes, and we welcome everyone. Click the button below to learn more about the ways we can support you and your business.
